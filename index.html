App Description

Echoes Within is a unique visual novel that allows players to experience life through the lens of someone with Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). The game offers an immersive experience where players can navigate through various life scenarios, making decisions that impact the storyline. In addition to the narrative, Echoes Within includes a music therapy section, providing a selection of calming songs to enhance the player's experience. The game also features an AI chat box, offering players the opportunity to ask questions and learn more about DID and related topics. With its engaging storyline and educational elements, Echoes Within provides a thought-provoking and enlightening experience for players.
